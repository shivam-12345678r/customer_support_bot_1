Resetting Your Password
To reset your password, go to the login page and click "Forgot Password." Enter your email and follow the link sent to you. If you don't receive an email, check your spam folder or contact support.

Refund Policy
We offer refunds within 30 days of purchase. Contact support at support@example.com with your order number to start the process. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after approval.

Contacting Support
Email us at support@example.com or call 1-800-555-1234 during business hours (9 AM - 5 PM EST). Live chat support is available on our website for urgent issues.

Shipping Information
Orders are processed within 2-3 business days. Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Expedited shipping options are available at checkout. You will receive a tracking number via email once your order is shipped.

Product Warranty
Our products come with a one-year warranty covering manufacturing defects. To claim a warranty, contact support with your proof of purchase. Accidental damage is not covered under warranty.

Account Deletion
To delete your account, navigate to the account settings page and request account deletion. This process is irreversible, and all your data will be permanently removed.

Payment Methods
We accept Visa, MasterCard, PayPal, and Apple Pay. Transactions are securely processed, and your payment details are not stored on our servers.

Subscription Cancellation
To cancel your subscription, go to your account settings and click on "Manage Subscriptions." Cancellations must be made at least 48 hours before the next billing cycle.

Technical Support
For technical issues, restart your device and update your software. If the issue persists, contact support with a detailed description and screenshots.

Privacy Policy
We value your privacy. Your personal data is stored securely and never shared with third parties without your consent. Read our full privacy policy at www.example.com/privacy.
